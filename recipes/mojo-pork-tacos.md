# Mojo Pork Tacos (500 g Pork Tenderloin)

**Category:** Mains / Tacos  
**Tags:** pork, tacos, Cuban, mojo, citrus, weeknight  
**Serves:** 4 (8–10 tacos)  
**Created:** 2025-10-28

## Summary
Bright, garlicky Cuban-style mojo on quick-cooking pork tenderloin, sliced and tucked into warm tortillas with fresh toppings.

## Ingredients

### Mojo Marinade
- 60 ml (1/4 cup) fresh orange juice
- 30 ml (2 tbsp) fresh lime juice
- 2 cloves garlic, minced or grated
- 2 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
- 1/2 tsp ground cumin
- 1/2 tsp dried oregano
- 1/4 tsp kosher salt (to taste)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Optional: 1/4 tsp black pepper, pinch chili flakes

### Pork
- 500 g pork tenderloin, trimmed of silver skin
- 1 tbsp neutral oil (for searing; omit if grilling)

### To Serve
- 8–10 small corn or flour tortillas, warmed
- Pickled red onions
- Fresh cilantro leaves
- Avocado slices or guacamole
- Salsa verde or fresh tomato salsa
- Lime wedges

## Method

1. **Marinate (30–60 min, up to overnight):** Whisk marinade ingredients. Add pork, coat well, and refrigerate.
2. **Cook the pork:**  
   - **Grill:** Medium-high heat; grill 3–4 min per side until internal temp reaches 63 °C / 145 °F.  
   - **Skillet + Oven:** Sear in hot skillet with 1 tbsp oil, 2–3 min per side; finish at 200 °C / 400 °F for 8–10 min to 63 °C / 145 °F.
3. **Rest & slice:** Rest 5 min; slice thinly across the grain.
4. **Assemble tacos:** Fill warm tortillas with pork and toppings. Squeeze lime to finish.

## Notes
- Don’t overcook tenderloin—pull at 63 °C / 145 °F for juicy slices.
- For more punch, add 1 tsp orange zest and 1 small grated garlic clove to the tortillas as you warm them.
- Make-ahead: marinate up to 24 hours. Leftovers keep 3 days; reheat gently.
