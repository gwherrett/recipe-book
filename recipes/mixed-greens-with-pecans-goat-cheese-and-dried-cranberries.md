# Mixed Greens with Pecans, Goat Cheese, and Dried Cranberries

**Source:** Bon App√©tit, October 2003 - Entertaining with Style

A classic salad combination featuring mixed greens, sweet dried cranberries, tangy goat cheese, and glazed pecans with a red wine vinaigrette. Perfect for entertaining.

**Servings:** 10

---

## Ingredients

### For the Dressing:
- 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
- 3/4 cup olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

### For the Salad:
- 2 5-ounce bags mixed baby greens
- 1 1/2 cups dried cranberries (about 7 ounces)
- 1 small red onion, very thinly sliced
- 1 1/2 cups glazed or honey-roasted pecans or walnuts (about 8 ounces)
- 1 5.5-ounce log soft fresh goat cheese, crumbled (about 1 1/4 cups)

---

## Instructions

1. **Make the dressing:**
   Whisk vinegar, mustard, and thyme in small bowl. Gradually whisk in oil. Season dressing with salt and pepper.

2. **Assemble the salad:**
   Mix greens, cranberries, and onion in large bowl. Mix in enough dressing to coat. Sprinkle with nuts and cheese.

3. **Serve:**
   Serve immediately for best texture.

---

## Notes

- Great for holiday entertaining or dinner parties
- Can prepare dressing up to 1 day ahead and refrigerate
- Add nuts and cheese just before serving to maintain crunch and prevent wilting
- Reserve any extra dressing on the side
