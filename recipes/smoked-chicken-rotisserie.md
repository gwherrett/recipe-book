# Rotisserie Chicken – Two Ways

## 1. Classic Italian Rotisserie Chicken

**Flavour Profile**: Herbaceous, garlicky, citrusy

### Marinade (overnight or at least 4 hours)
- 3 tbsp olive oil  
- Juice of 1 lemon  
- Zest of 1 lemon  
- 4 cloves garlic, minced  
- 1 tbsp chopped rosemary  
- 1 tbsp chopped thyme  
- 1 tbsp chopped oregano  
- Salt and cracked black pepper  

### Optional Stuffing
- Half a lemon
- A few garlic cloves
- Sprigs of rosemary (in the cavity)

### Baste During Cook
- Olive oil  
- Lemon juice  
- Rosemary sprig

---

## 2. Peri-Peri Style Rotisserie Chicken with BBQ Twist

**Flavour Profile**: Spicy, tangy, smoky

### Peri-Peri Base Marinade
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- 2 tbsp vinegar (red wine or white)  
- Juice of 1 lemon  
- 4 garlic cloves  
- 2 tsp smoked paprika  
- 1–2 tsp chili flakes (or fresh red chilies)  
- 1 tsp dried oregano  
- Salt and pepper

### BBQ Sauce Integration Options
- Add 2 tbsp of your BBQ sauce *into* the marinade to mellow acidity and introduce smoke/sweet
- OR: Glaze with a mix of 2 parts peri-peri marinade and 1 part BBQ sauce in the last 20 minutes of cooking
- Optional: Brush extra BBQ sauce glaze in the final 5–10 minutes for a glossy finish

---

## Cooking Tips
- Truss chickens tightly for even rotisserie rotation  
- Cook indirect at ~375°F (190°C) for 1.25 to 1.5 hours depending on size  
- Target internal temp: 165°F (74°C) in the breast, 175°F (80°C) in the thigh

---

*Enjoy with roasted potatoes, a crisp salad, or warm flatbreads and dips!*
