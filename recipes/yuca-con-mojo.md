# Yucca with Garlic Sauce (Yuca con Mojo)

**Source:** La Fontana, Havana

**Yield:** 4 servings

A distinctly Cuban side dish discovered at La Fontana, a *paladar* — a privately run home-restaurant — in Havana. Boiled yucca wedges are drizzled with a warm garlic, scallion, and lime sauce.

---

## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. yucca, halved crosswise, peeled, and cut into thick wedges
- Salt
- ¼ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- 3 scallions, trimmed and minced
- 3 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
- 1 tbsp. chopped fresh parsley
- Juice of 1 lime

---

## Instructions

1. **Boil the yucca:** Put yucca in a medium pot, cover with cold water by 2", and add a pinch of salt. Simmer over medium-high heat until very soft and slightly translucent, 30–40 minutes. Drain, then arrange on a platter.

2. **Make the garlic sauce:** Meanwhile, heat olive and vegetable oils in a medium skillet over medium heat. Add scallions and garlic, season to taste with salt, and cook, stirring often, until soft, about 5 minutes. Remove skillet from heat and stir in parsley and lime juice. Serve yucca drizzled with the warm garlic sauce.
