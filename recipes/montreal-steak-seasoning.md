# Montreal Steak Seasoning

**Category:** Spice Blends\
**Tags:** Steak, Grill, Canadian, Dry Rub

**Summary:**\
A bold, aromatic blend inspired by Montreal delis --- perfect for
seasoning steaks, burgers, or grilled vegetables. This mix balances
peppery heat with garlic, coriander, and dill for that classic
smoky-savory crust.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Ingredients

-   2 tbsp coarse kosher salt\
-   2 tbsp black peppercorns, coarsely crushed\
-   1 tbsp paprika\
-   1 tbsp granulated garlic\
-   1 tbsp granulated onion\
-   1 tbsp crushed coriander seeds\
-   1 tbsp dill seed (or dried dill weed)\
-   1 tbsp red pepper flakes (adjust for heat)\
-   1 tbsp fennel seed *(optional twist for subtle sweetness)*

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Method

1.  Lightly crush peppercorns, coriander, dill, and fennel seeds using a
    mortar and pestle or spice grinder.\
2.  Combine with remaining ingredients in a small bowl and mix
    thoroughly.\
3.  Transfer to an airtight jar and store in a cool, dark place for up
    to 6 months.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## To Use

-   Pat steaks dry and coat liberally with seasoning before grilling or
    broiling.\
-   Allow to rest 30--60 minutes before cooking to let the salt draw in
    flavor.\
-   Also great on pork, chicken, or grilled vegetables.
