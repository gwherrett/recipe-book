## Summary
A bold, smoky, and slightly spicy Cajun-style preparation for wild sockeye salmon. The spice rub creates a beautifully charred crust while keeping the interior tender and juicy. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a special meal served with rice, roasted vegetables, or a bright salad.

### Blackening Spice Mix
- 2 tsp paprika (smoked or sweet)  
- 1 tsp cayenne pepper *(adjust for spice level)*

- 1 tsp black pepper

- 1/2 tsp brown sugar *(optional, for deeper crust)*
