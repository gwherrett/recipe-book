# Three-Layer Causa Rellena (Peruvian Potato Terrine)

## Ingredients

### For the Potato Base
- 6 medium-sized yellow potatoes (Yukon Gold or Peruvian yellow potatoes)
- 3 tbsp vegetable oil
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 2 tsp aji amarillo paste (yellow chili paste)
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Layer 1: Chicken or Tuna Mix
- 2 cups shredded chicken or canned tuna
- 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- 1/2 red onion, finely diced
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- 1/2 tsp aji amarillo paste
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Layer 2: Cheese and Sausage Mix
- 1 cup shredded mozzarella or queso fresco
- 1 cup cooked and crumbled sausage (e.g., chorizo or mild Italian sausage)
- 1/4 cup cream cheese or mayonnaise (for binding)

### Layer 3: Avocado and Tomato
- 2 avocados, sliced or mashed (tossed with lime juice to prevent browning)
- 2 medium tomatoes, sliced thinly or diced
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Garnish (Optional)
- Sliced boiled eggs
- Black olives
- Chopped parsley or cilantro

---

## Instructions

1. **Cook the Potatoes:**  
   Peel and boil the potatoes in salted water until soft (about 20â€“25 minutes).  
   Drain and mash while warm until smooth and lump-free.

2. **Make the Potato Base:**  
   Mix mashed potatoes with vegetable oil, lime juice, aji amarillo paste, salt, and pepper.  
   Knead until smooth and let cool.

3. **Prepare the Layers:**
   - **Chicken/Tuna Mix:** Combine shredded chicken or tuna with mayonnaise, lime juice, red onion, aji amarillo paste, salt, and pepper.
   - **Cheese and Sausage Mix:** Mix cooked sausage with shredded cheese and cream cheese or mayo until well blended.
   - **Avocado and Tomato Layer:** Slice or mash avocados and toss with lime juice. Prepare tomatoes, seasoned with salt and pepper.

4. **Assemble the Causa:**
   Use a ring mold or small baking dish to layer:
   - Bottom layer: 1/3 of the potato mixture
   - First layer: Chicken or tuna mix
   - Middle layer: 1/3 of the potato mixture
   - Second layer: Cheese and sausage mix
   - Top layer: Remaining potato mixture
   - Final topping: Avocado and tomato layer

5. **Garnish:**  
   Top with slices of boiled egg, black olives, and fresh herbs.

6. **Chill and Serve:**  
   Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before slicing.  
   Serve cold as an appetizer or light main.

---

## Notes
- Aji amarillo paste is key for authentic flavor. Substitute with mild chili paste if unavailable.
- The layers can be customized: swap protein, make vegetarian, or add pickled onions.
