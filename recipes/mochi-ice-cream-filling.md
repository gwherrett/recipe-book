# Mochi (Ice Cream Filling)

Simple microwave mochi dough made with mochiko rice flour, sugar, and water — ready to wrap around your favourite ice cream filling.

---

## Ingredients

- 150g mochiko rice flour
- 70g sugar
- 250g water
- Cornstarch, for coating

---

## Instructions

1. **Mix:** Combine mochiko rice flour, sugar, and water in a microwave-safe bowl and stir until smooth.

2. **Microwave:** Microwave on high for 2 minutes, stir, then microwave for a further 2 minutes.

3. **Coat and shape:** Dust a surface generously with cornstarch, turn the mochi out, and shape as desired around ice cream filling.
