## Mint Chutney (Pudina Chutney)
**Category:** Condiments & Sauces  
**Tags:** Indian, Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten-Free  

### Summary  
A bright, tangy, and herbal chutney that pairs perfectly with samosas, pakoras, kebabs, or grilled meats. This is the traditional North Indian style — fresh, green, and bursting with mint and cilantro flavor.

---

### Ingredients  
- 1 cup fresh mint leaves (tightly packed)  
- 1 cup fresh cilantro leaves (tightly packed)  
- 1–2 green chilies (adjust to spice preference)  
- 1 small onion or 1 tbsp chopped shallot (optional, adds body)  
- 1-inch piece fresh ginger  
- Juice of ½–1 lime (or lemon)  
- ½ tsp roasted cumin powder  
- ½ tsp salt (adjust to taste)  
- 1–2 tsp sugar (optional, balances flavor)  
- 2–3 tbsp cold water (to blend)  

**Optional Variations:**  
- Add 2 tbsp plain yogurt for a creamier texture.  
- Add 1 tsp tamarind paste for a tangy-sweet twist.  

---

### Method  
1. Combine all ingredients in a blender or small food processor.  
2. Blend until smooth, adding cold water gradually to adjust consistency.  
3. Taste and adjust salt, sugar, and lime juice as needed.  
4. Serve immediately or refrigerate in an airtight jar for up to 3–4 days.
