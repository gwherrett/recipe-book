# Aloo Gobi

**Category:** Indian / Vegetarian  
**Summary:** Classic North Indian potato–cauliflower stir fry with onions, ginger, garlic, spices, and coriander.

## Ingredients
- 4 potatoes, peeled and chopped into bite‑size pieces  
- 1 small cauliflower, cut into florets  
- ½ onion, finely chopped  
- ½ onion, chopped into large chunks  
- ½ green pepper, finely chopped  
- 2 tbsp tomato purée  
- 2 inches root ginger, grated  
- 4 cloves garlic, sliced  
- 2 tsp curry powder  
- 1 tsp cumin powder  
- 1 tsp chilli powder  
- 1 tsp turmeric  
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce  
- 1 tomato, quartered  
- 5 tbsp chopped coriander leaves  
- Vegetable oil  
- Salt (to taste)

## Method
1. Boil potatoes and cauliflower in salted water with a little turmeric until cooked.  
2. In vegetable oil, fry the finely chopped onion until translucent.  
3. Add curry powder, cumin powder, sliced garlic, and grated ginger; stir‑fry 2 minutes.  
4. Add pepper, tomato, tomato purée, onion chunks, and Worcestershire sauce; stir‑fry 2 minutes.  
5. Add potatoes and cauliflower; mix well to coat with sauce.  
6. Serve immediately topped with chopped coriander.
