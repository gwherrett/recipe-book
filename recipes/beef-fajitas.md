# Beef Fajitas

**Category:** Mains → Tex-Mex\
**Tags:** beef, fajitas, Mexican, skillet

## Ingredients

-   1½ lbs (680 g) flank steak, thinly sliced against the grain\
-   3 bell peppers (1 red, 1 green, 1 yellow), thinly sliced\
-   1 large onion, thinly sliced\
-   ¼ cup (60 ml) fresh lime juice\
-   ¼ cup (60 ml) olive oil\
-   3 cloves garlic, minced\
-   1 tsp ground cumin\
-   1 tsp chili powder\
-   ½ tsp smoked paprika\
-   ½ tsp oregano\
-   Salt and pepper to taste\
-   8--10 small flour or corn tortillas\
-   Fresh cilantro, for garnish\
-   *Optional toppings:* sour cream, guacamole, pico de gallo, shredded
    cheese, lime wedges

## Instructions

1.  **Prepare the marinade:** Combine lime juice, olive oil, garlic,
    cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper in a
    small bowl.\
2.  **Marinate the steak:** Place steak in a resealable bag or dish,
    pour marinade over it, and refrigerate for 1--8 hours.\
3.  **Cook the steak:** Heat a large skillet or grill pan over
    medium-high heat. Cook steak 2--3 minutes per side until desired
    doneness. Remove and rest under foil.\
4.  **Sauté vegetables:** In the same skillet, cook bell peppers and
    onions 5--7 minutes until tender and slightly charred. Season to
    taste.\
5.  **Warm tortillas:** Heat tortillas briefly in a dry skillet or
    microwave until pliable.\
6.  **Assemble fajitas:** Layer steak and vegetables on tortillas, top
    with cilantro and desired toppings.

**Serves:** 4\
**Time:** 45 minutes (plus marinating time)\
**Notes:** Great with grilled corn, black beans, or Mexican rice.
