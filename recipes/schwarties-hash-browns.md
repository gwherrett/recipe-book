# Schwarties Hash Browns

**Source:** [Best of Bridge](https://www.bestofbridge.com/hash-brown-potatoes-schwarties/)

**Cook Time:** 60 minutes
**Yield:** 4–5 servings

A Best of Bridge classic holiday casserole. Great for buffets and freezes well.

---

## Ingredients

- 1 lb. (500 g) frozen hash brown potatoes
- 1 cup (250 mL) sour cream
- 1 × 10 oz. (284 mL) can mushroom soup
- ¼ cup melted butter
- Grated onion to taste
- Salt to taste
- 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
- 1 Tbsp. parmesan cheese

---

## Instructions

1. **Thaw potatoes:** Thaw potatoes slightly.

2. **Mix and bake:** Mix potatoes, sour cream, mushroom soup, butter, onion, salt and cheddar cheese in a baking dish. Sprinkle parmesan on top. Bake at 350°F for 1 hour.
