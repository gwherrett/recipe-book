# Thai Dressing

A bright, tangy, and savory Thai-style dressing perfect for salads, grilled meats, or as a dipping sauce.

## Ingredients
- 1/4 cup fish sauce
- 1/4 cup lime juice
- 1/4 cup water  
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1–2 red Thai chilies, finely chopped
- 2 Tbsp brown sugar
- 1 Tbsp soy sauce
- 1 Tbsp rice vinegar
- 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
- 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
- 1 Tbsp green onion, chopped
- 1–2 red Thai chilies, finely chopped

## Instructions
1. In a small bowl, combine the lime juice, fish sauce, and brown sugar. Stir until the sugar is fully dissolved.
2. Add the minced garlic, chopped Thai chilies, soy sauce, and rice vinegar. Mix well.
3. Stir in the chopped cilantro and mint.
4. Taste and adjust the ingredients to your preference.
5. Use immediately as a salad dressing or dipping sauce, or refrigerate for up to 3 days.

## Notes
- Add more lime juice for extra tang.
- Substitute honey for brown sugar if desired.
- Great with Thai salads, grilled shrimp, or rice noodle bowls.
