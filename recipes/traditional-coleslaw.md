## Ingredients

- 4 cups green cabbage, finely shredded  
- 1 cup red cabbage, finely shredded (optional for color)  
- 1 cup carrots, grated  
- ½ cup mayonnaise  
- 1½ tablespoons apple cider vinegar (or white vinegar)  
- 1 tablespoon sugar (adjust to taste)  
- ½ teaspoon salt  
- ¼ teaspoon black pepper  
- ½ teaspoon celery seed (optional)


## Method

1. **Prepare the Vegetables** – In a large bowl, combine the shredded cabbage and grated carrots.  
2. **Make the Dressing** – In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, salt, black pepper, and celery seed (if using).  
3. **Combine** – Pour the dressing over the cabbage and carrots, tossing well to coat evenly.  
4. **Chill** – Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow flavors to meld.  
5. **Serve** – Give it a final toss before serving. Enjoy as a side for BBQ, fried chicken, or sandwiches!
