# Meatballs and Peas in Tomato Sauce (Pork & Lamb Sausage, 800 g)

**Category:** Mains → Meat & Poultry  
**Tags:** Mediterranean, pork, lamb, tomato, baked, feta  
**Serves:** 6 (or 4 generous portions)

A rich and aromatic baked meatball dish that balances the sweetness of peas and tomatoes with the warmth of cinnamon and allspice. Using lamb sausage deepens the flavor, while pork keeps the texture tender. Serve with rice, mashed potatoes, or crusty bread.

---

## Ingredients

### Meatballs
- 3–3½ slices white bread, crusts removed, torn (≈ 95–100 g)  
- 5 Tbsp (≈ 75 ml) whole milk  
- 1 onion, roughly chopped (≈ 225 g / 1½ cups)  
- 6 garlic cloves, crushed  
- **400 g ground pork**  
- **400 g lamb sausage**, casings removed  
- 3¼ tsp ground cinnamon (≈ 1 Tbsp + ¼ tsp)  
- 1½ tsp ground allspice + pinch  
- 1½ cups parsley (≈ 24 g), roughly chopped + extra to serve  
- ⅓ cup mint leaves (≈ 16 g), roughly chopped  
- Zest of 1–2 lemons (≈ 2½ tsp)  
- Heaped ½ cup pitted green olives (≈ 110–115 g), roughly chopped  
- 2 tsp kosher salt (to taste; lamb sausage adds salt)  
- Black pepper  
- Olive oil for frying (≈ 3 Tbsp + 1 tsp)

### Sauce
- 1½ Tbsp olive oil (+ 1½ tsp for drizzling)  
- 1 onion, finely chopped (≈ 240 g)  
- 6 garlic cloves, crushed  
- 1½ tsp cumin seeds, crushed  
- 9–10 cardamom pods, lightly crushed  
- 1 cinnamon stick  
- ¾ tsp chili flakes  
- 3 Tbsp + 1 tsp tomato paste  
- 960 g grated plum tomatoes (≈ 6 cups)  
- 380–400 ml chicken stock  
- 2½ tsp sugar  
- 2¼ tsp kosher salt  
- Black pepper  
- 480 g peas (≈ 4 cups)  
- 240 g feta  
- 3–4 green onions (≈ 40 g)

---

## Method
1. Soak bread in milk.  
2. Mix meatballs gently and form ~36 balls (~40 g each).  
3. Brown in batches.  
4. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).  
5. Build sauce, reduce by one-third, stir in peas.  
6. Transfer sauce to baking dish, nestle in meatballs, top with feta, drizzle oil.  
7. Bake 22–25 minutes.  
8. Rest 10 minutes, top with green onions and parsley, serve.

---

## Notes
- Lamb sausage is salty—taste and adjust salt at the end.  
- Sauce keeps 3 days refrigerated.  
- Do not overmix meatballs for tenderness.
