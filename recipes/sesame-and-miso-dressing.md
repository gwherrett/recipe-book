# Sesame and Miso Dressing

**Source:** [Yotam Ottolenghi's Crowd Pleaser, Menu 2: The Weeknight Dinner](https://www.channel4.com/programmes/ottolenghis-crowd-pleasers)

**Prep Time:** 5 minutes
**Assembly Time:** 15 minutes
**Yield:** 6 servings

A nutty, umami-rich emulsified dressing of white miso, sesame oil, palm sugar, rice vinegar, and lime — finished with toasted coriander, black, and white sesame seeds.

---

## Ingredients

- 30g white miso
- 1 garlic clove, crushed
- 2 tsp sesame oil
- 20g palm sugar (or brown sugar)
- 1½ tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- 60ml good-quality rapeseed oil (or sunflower oil)
- 1½ tsp coriander seeds, lightly ground in a pestle and mortar, toasted
- 1½ tsp black sesame seeds, toasted
- 1½ tsp white sesame seeds, toasted

---

## Instructions

1. **Make the base:** Whisk together the miso, garlic, sesame oil, sugar, rice vinegar, and lime juice in a bowl with 1 tablespoon of water.

2. **Emulsify:** Slowly drizzle in the rapeseed oil whilst whisking constantly until emulsified.

3. **Finish:** Stir in all the seeds.
