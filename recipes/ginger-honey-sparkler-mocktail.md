# Ginger–Honey Sparkler (Mocktail)

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup honey  
- 1/2 cup peeled fresh ginger, sliced  
- 1/2 cup water  
- 4 cups club soda  
- Fresh mint, to taste  
- Ice  

## Method
1. Combine honey, sliced ginger, and water in a small saucepan.  
2. Bring to a simmer over medium heat and cook, stirring occasionally, until the flavors meld — about 15 minutes.  
3. Remove from heat and let cool.  
4. Strain the ginger–honey syrup into a large pitcher.  
5. Add the club soda, mint, and ice. Stir gently to combine.  
6. Divide among 8-ounce glasses and serve.
