# Asian Grilled Steak Salad

**Source:** HeartSmart™ - The Best of HeartSmart™ Cooking by Bonnie Stern and the Heart and Stroke Foundation (Summer 2008)

This nutrient-rich main course salad features Health Check™ qualified lean beef and a delicious NO-fat dressing. You can substitute any thin-cut Beef Marinating or Grilling steak or medallions for the flank steak.

**Servings:** 6

---

## Ingredients

### For the Marinade:
- ½ bunch fresh cilantro, including roots, stems and leaves
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 tbsp (15 mL) minced ginger root
- 2 tbsp (25 mL) EACH hoisin sauce, soy sauce and fresh lemon juice
- 1 tsp (5 mL) Asian chili paste
- 1 lb (500 mL) Beef Flank Marinating Steak

### For the Salad:
- 10 cups (2.5 L) mixed salad greens
- 1 large English cucumber, thinly sliced
- ½ cup (125 mL) coarsely chopped fresh cilantro or parsley
- ¼ cup (50 mL) EACH chopped fresh mint and chives or green onions

### For the Citrus Dressing:
- ⅓ cup (75 mL) EACH granulated sugar and water
- ⅓ cup (75 mL) shredded carrot
- 2 tbsp (25 mL) EACH rice or cider vinegar and orange juice
- 2 tbsp (30 mL) EACH fresh lemon juice and soy sauce
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ½ tsp (2 mL) hot Asian chili paste

---

## Directions

1. **Prepare marinade:**
   Combine the half bunch cilantro, garlic, ginger root, hoisin sauce, soy sauce, lemon juice and chili paste in food processor. Blend until smooth puree consistency.

2. **Marinate steak:**
   Pour into large sealable freezer bag along with meat. Press out any excess air. Refrigerate for 1 hour or up to 12 hours.

3. **Grill steak:**
   Discard marinade. Pat steak dry with paper towel and grill over medium-high heat for 4 to 5 minutes per side or until medium-rare. Let stand 10-15 minutes before carving into thin slices on diagonal.

4. **Make citrus dressing:**
   Combine ⅓ cup (75 mL) EACH granulated sugar and water in small saucepan. Cook over high heat a few minutes until sugar dissolves. Remove from heat, stir in ⅓ cup (75 mL) shredded carrot, 2 tbsp (25 mL) EACH rice or cider vinegar and orange juice, 2 tbsp (30 mL) EACH fresh lemon juice and soy sauce, 1 clove garlic (minced) and ½ tsp (2 mL) hot Asian chili paste.

5. **Arrange salad:**
   Arrange salad greens on flat dish. Arrange cucumber on top and sprinkle with cilantro, mint and chives. Place steak slices on top and drizzle with Citrus Dressing.
