# Peanut Butter Cookies

---

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons softened unsalted butter
- 3 tablespoons peanut butter
- 3 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 large egg yolk
- ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
- ¾ cup plus 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- ¼ teaspoon baking soda
- Pinch salt

---

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven:** 350°F.

2. **Mix dry ingredients:** Whisk together flour, salt, and baking soda in a bowl.

3. **Cream butter and sugars:** In a medium bowl, combine butter, peanut butter, brown sugar, and sugar until fully mixed.

4. **Add egg and vanilla:** Mix egg yolk and vanilla extract into the butter mixture.

5. **Combine:** Add the flour to wet ingredients. Stir.
