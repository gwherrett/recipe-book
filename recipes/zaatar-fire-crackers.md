# Za'atar Fire Crackers

**Active Time:** 10 minutes
**Total Time:** 30 minutes, plus 6 hours marinating
**Yield:** Serves 8

Toasted plain oyster crackers get a flavor boost from warm spices plus brightness from the sumac in Lior Lev Sercarz's Za'atar Spice Blend. These crackers make an excellent snack on their own, but they are also worth adding to your next charcuterie board or as a garnish for soups and salads.

---

## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup olive oil
- 3 Tbsp. Za'atar Spice Blend
- 1 Tbsp. crushed red pepper
- 1 tsp. garlic powder
- 1 (9-oz.) pkg. oyster crackers
- Kosher salt (optional)

---

## Instructions

1. **Marinate:** Combine olive oil, za'atar spice blend, crushed red pepper, and garlic powder in a 1-gallon ziplock plastic bag, and gently massage bag to combine. Add crackers to bag, and shake until crackers are evenly coated, about 30 seconds. Press out any excess air, and seal bag. Lay crackers in an even layer, and lay flat on a work surface. Marinate at room temperature, shaking bag occasionally, until seasonings adhere to crackers, at least 6 hours.

2. **Bake:** Preheat oven to 250°F. Remove crackers from bag, and spread in an even layer on a large rimmed baking sheet. Sprinkle remaining spice mixture from bag over crackers. Bake in preheated oven until crackers are light golden brown and toasted, about 15 minutes. Season with salt to taste, if desired. —CHANDRA RAM

---

## Make Ahead

Za'atar Fire Crackers are delicious right out of the oven but can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature.
