**Summary:**  
Tender, juicy pulled pork made with a balanced spice rub and a tangy, slightly sweet cooking sauce. Ideal for sandwiches, tacos, or rice bowls. The recipe includes both slow cooker and Instant Pot methods to ensure perfect texture and flavor.

### Instructions:

#### 1. Prepare the Pork  
Mix paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper, cayenne, and brown sugar. Rub evenly over the pork shoulder.  

#### 2. Add Aromatics  
Place onion and garlic in the slow cooker or Instant Pot base. Add the seasoned pork on top.

#### 3. Add Liquids  
Pour in the broth, vinegar, and Worcestershire sauce.  

---

### **Slow Cooker Method**  
- Cook on **LOW for 8‚Äì10 hours** or **HIGH for 5‚Äì6 hours**, until pork is tender and shreds easily.  

---

### **Instant Pot Method**  
- Set to **High Pressure for 60 minutes**, then **Natural Release for 15 minutes**.  
- If you prefer firmer texture, reduce cooking time to **45‚Äì50 minutes**.  

---

### **Shred & Finish**  
Remove pork and shred with two forks. Discard excess fat. Return to pot and mix with cooking juices.  
Add BBQ sauce to taste and stir well.  

---

### **Serve With:**  
- Soft buns and coleslaw  
- Tacos with pickled onions  
- Over rice with roasted vegetables  

---

### **Storage:**  
- Refrigerate up to 4 days, or freeze up to 3 months.
