# Chile Relleno

## Ingredients

### Chiles
- 4 pasilla chiles
- 1 lb queso fresco
- Toothpicks
- 3 eggs
- 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
- 1 cup oil (for frying)

### Tomato Sauce
- 4 medium Roma tomatoes, halved
- 1 cup water
- 2 garlic cloves, peeled and chopped
- 1 tbsp chicken broth powder
- 1/4 cup oil
- 1 tsp all-purpose flour
- 1 tbsp chopped fresh oregano

### Optional
- Rice and beans, for serving

## Directions

### Prepare the Chiles
1. Heat grill to medium.
2. Grill pasilla chiles until blackened on both sides.
3. Transfer to a sealed plastic bag and let steam for 10 minutes.
4. Remove from bag, slit each chile down the side, and remove seeds.
5. Stuff with queso fresco and secure with toothpicks.

### Batter
6. Separate egg yolks and whites.
7. Beat egg whites until fluffy.
8. Add flour and egg yolks; mix until incorporated.

### Fry
9. Heat 1 cup oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
10. Dip stuffed chiles into batter to coat.
11. Fry until golden brown on all sides.
12. Transfer to a serving platter.

## Tomato Sauce
13. Add tomatoes and water to a pot; simmer until tomatoes soften.
14. Stir in garlic and chicken broth powder.
15. In a separate pan, heat 1/4 cup oil over low heat.
16. Add 1 tsp flour to form a roux; cook until lightly browned.
17. Add tomato mixture into the roux and simmer 5 minutes.
18. Stir in chopped oregano.

## Serve
19. Pour tomato sauce over the fried chile rellenos.
20. Serve with rice and beans if desired.
