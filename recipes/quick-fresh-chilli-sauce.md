# Quick Fresh Chilli Sauce

**Source:** [Yotam Ottolenghi's Crowd Pleaser, Menu 2: The Weeknight Dinner](https://www.channel4.com/programmes/ottolenghis-crowd-pleasers)

**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Yield:** 6 servings

A rough, vibrant fresh chilli sauce made in minutes — mild red chillies blended with cherry tomatoes, cider vinegar, and olive oil. Keep it coarse for texture.

---

## Ingredients

- 8 mild red chillies, roughly chopped (150g)
- 3 cherry tomatoes
- 1 tbsp cider vinegar
- 1 tsp fine sea salt
- 2 tbsp olive oil

---

## Instructions

1. **Blend:** Add the chillies, tomatoes, vinegar, and salt to a small blender or food processor. Pulse a few times to crush — do not over-blend; the sauce should retain some texture.

2. **Finish:** Transfer to a bowl and stir in the olive oil.
