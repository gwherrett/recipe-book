# Shrimp Cocktail Sauce

**Source:** [The Black Peppercorn](https://www.theblackpeppercorn.com/shrimp-cocktail-sauce/)

**Prep Time:** 5 minutes
**Yield:** 12 tablespoons

Homemade cocktail sauce for shrimp — quick, easy, and copycat to the Heinz seafood cocktail sauce.

---

## Ingredients

- ½ cup ketchup
- 2 tbsp horseradish (more or less to taste)
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp hot sauce
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce

---

## Instructions

1. **Mix:** Whisk all the ingredients together until well mixed.

2. **Serve:** Serve with cooked shrimp.
