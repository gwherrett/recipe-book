# Aromatic Rice

**Source:** [Yotam Ottolenghi's Crowd Pleaser, Menu 2: The Weeknight Dinner](https://www.channel4.com/programmes/ottolenghis-crowd-pleasers)

**Soaking Time:** 1 hour
**Prep Time:** 5 minutes
**Cook Time:** 25 minutes
**Resting Time:** 10 minutes
**Yield:** 4 servings

Buttery basmati rice toasted with broken angel hair pasta, cinnamon, cardamom, and lemon peel — inspired by Lebanese rice with vermicelli. Light, fragrant, and a perfect companion to braises and stews.

---

## Ingredients

- 280g basmati rice
- 50g unsalted butter
- 50g angel hair pasta (wheat vermicelli), broken
- 1 cinnamon stick
- 5 cardamom pods
- 4 strips of shaved lemon skin
- 1 tsp fine sea salt
- 500ml water

---

## Instructions

1. **Soak the rice:** Rinse the rice in a strainer under cold tap water until the water runs clear. Place in a bowl, cover with water, and soak for 1 hour. Drain well.

2. **Toast the aromatics:** Put the butter, broken angel hair pasta, cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, and lemon skin into a medium saucepan over medium-high heat. Cook for 3 minutes, stirring continuously, until golden-brown.

3. **Cook:** Mix in the drained rice and stir well. Add the water and salt and bring to the boil. Cover with a tight-fitting lid, reduce the heat to low, and cook gently for 20 minutes.

4. **Rest and serve:** Turn off the heat and leave covered for 10 minutes. Fluff with a fork and serve.
