# Ham Tetrazzini

**Yield:** Serves 4–6

Named for famous Italian opera singer Luisa Tetrazzini, this recipe has become a popular classic — egg noodles tossed with ham, sautéed vegetables, olives, and a rich cream sauce finished with egg yolks.

---

## Ingredients

### Tetrazzini

- 1½ cups sliced fresh mushrooms
- 1 green pepper, sliced
- 1 onion, finely sliced
- 3 to 4 tbsp. olive or salad oil
- 6 stuffed green olives, sliced
- 14 oz. egg noodles (green if possible)
- 2 cups cooked ham, in ½-inch cubes
- Grated Parmesan cheese

### Cream Sauce

- 6 tbsp. butter
- 6 tbsp. flour
- ½ tsp. salt
- 3 cups scalded milk
- Pinch of white pepper
- 2 egg yolks

---

## Instructions

1. **Make cream sauce:** Melt 6 tbsp. butter in a heavy saucepan over low heat and stir in 6 tbsp. flour with a whisk. Stir constantly until bubbly but not brown. Add ½ tsp. salt and 3 cups of scalded milk, continuing to stir. Cook 4–5 minutes over very low heat, add a pinch of white pepper. Mix 2 tbsp. of the sauce into 2 egg yolks, then pour them into sauce.

2. **Sauté vegetables:** Sauté first 3 ingredients in oil till tender.

3. **Combine:** Add olives, and combine with cream sauce in the top of a double boiler over low heat.

4. **Cook noodles:** Cook noodles according to package directions. Drain and place on hot, buttered serving dish.

5. **Finish:** Toss into noodles with ham, sprinkle with cheese and serve.
