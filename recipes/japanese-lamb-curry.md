# Japanese Lamb Curry with Vermont Mild Roux, Green Apple & Koji Salt

**Category:** Mains / Japanese  
**Tags:** Lamb, Leftovers, Curry Roux, Apple, Koji  

## Summary  
A rich Japanese-style curry using leftover medium-rare lamb, Vermont Mild curry roux, and a bhuna spice base enhanced with green apple and koji salt for balanced umami and sweetness. The lamb is added only at the end to stay tender and juicy.

---

## Ingredients

### Main  
- 1 kg leftover lamb leg (medium-rare), cubed ~2 cm  
- 2 medium onions (~300 g), sliced  
- 2 medium carrots (~250 g), cut on bias into 1 cm pieces  
- 2–3 medium potatoes (~400 g), peeled & cubed ~2 cm  
- 4 cups (1 L) water or light stock (lamb/chicken/veg)  
- ½ box (100 g) Vermont Curry Mild roux (4 blocks)  
- 1–2 tbsp neutral oil or ghee  

### Bhuna Spice Base  
- 1 tsp ground cumin  
- ¾ tsp ground coriander  
- ½ tsp ground cardamom  
- ½ tsp ground cinnamon  
- ¼ tsp ground cloves  
- ¼ tsp ground nutmeg  
- ¼ tsp black pepper  
- ½–1 tsp hot chili powder or cayenne (to taste)  
- ½ tsp turmeric (optional for color and warmth)  

### Flavor Finishers  
- ½ grated green apple (≈ 50 g)  
- 1 tsp koji salt (or ½ tsp regular salt + ½ tsp miso if substituting)  
- 1 tsp soy sauce  
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce  
- 1 tsp honey (optional)

---

## Method

1️⃣ **Sauté and Bhuna**  
1. Heat oil/ghee in a large pot over medium-high heat.  
2. Add onions with a pinch of salt and cook 10–12 min until deep golden.  
3. Add carrots and sauté 3–4 min to lightly caramelize.  
4. Reduce heat and add all bhuna spices. Cook 1 min until fragrant and darkened.

2️⃣ **Simmer the Curry Base**  
5. Add potatoes, grated apple, and 4 cups water/stock.  
6. Bring to a gentle boil, then simmer 10–12 min until potatoes are just tender.  
7. Stir in koji salt, soy, and Worcestershire; taste for balance.

3️⃣ **Add Roux and Finish**  
8. Turn off heat and add roux blocks; stir to dissolve completely.  
9. Return to low heat and simmer 5 min until the sauce thickens and glosses.

4️⃣ **Warm the Lamb Gently**  
10. Add cubed lamb and stir to coat.  
11. Simmer gently 2–3 min until just heated through.  
12. Remove from heat and rest 5–10 min covered to meld flavors.

---

## Chef Tips  
- Add a knob of butter or a splash of cream for silky texture.  
- Finish with a few drops of sesame oil off heat for aroma.  
- Top with toasted sesame seeds or chopped parsley to serve.
