# Chicken Mole (Oaxacan-Style)

**Serves:** 4  
**Active Time:** 1 hour  
**Total Time:** 2 hours  
**Source:** Sydney Oland (Jan 14, 2012)

Mole varies widely in color and flavor, but all versions center around chilies.  
This Oaxacan-style mole blends dried chilies, spices, nuts, and chocolate into a deeply complex, rich sauce.  
Despite the unexpected combination of chicken and chocolate—trust the process. It's worth it.

---

## Ingredients

### Chilies & Spices
- 2 dried ancho chilies
- 3 dried New Mexico or guajillo chilies, stemmed and seeded
- 1/4 cup sesame seeds
- 1/2 tsp anise seeds
- 1/4 tsp cumin seeds
- 1/4 tsp coriander seeds
- 6 black peppercorns
- One 1/2-inch cinnamon stick

### Sauce Base
- 3 tbsp lard
- 1 1/2 tbsp raisins
- 10 whole almonds
- 2 plum tomatoes, finely chopped
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 2 1/2 cups chicken stock or low-sodium broth
- 1 1/2 oz Mexican chocolate, coarsely chopped
- Salt and freshly ground pepper

### Chicken
- 1 whole chicken (3–4 lb), cut into 8 pieces
- Salt & pepper
- 1 tbsp chopped cilantro (garnish)
- Lime wedges (optional)

---

## Instructions

### 1. Soften the Chilies
Place all dried chilies in a bowl and cover with hot water.  
Let soak for 30 minutes.

### 2. Toast & Grind the Spices
Toast sesame seeds, anise, cumin, coriander, peppercorns, and cinnamon stick in a dry skillet over low heat until fragrant (about 2 minutes).  
Cool and grind to a fine powder.

### 3. Fry Raisins & Almonds
Melt 1 tbsp lard. Add raisins and almonds.  
Cook 5 minutes until raisins plump and almonds brown. Transfer to a bowl.

### 4. Build the Mole Base
Melt 1 tbsp lard. Add tomatoes, garlic, onions, and ground spice mixture.  
Cook 7 minutes until bubbling.  
Add chilies and stock, partially cover, and simmer 1 hour.  
Blend with chocolate until smooth. Season with salt and pepper.

### 5. Cook the Chicken
Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).  
Season chicken. Brown in 1 tbsp lard.  
Pour mole over chicken, bring to a simmer.  
Cover and bake 45 minutes until tender.

---

## Serving
Serve with rice, beans, cilantro, and lime wedges.
