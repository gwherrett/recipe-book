# Shredded Radish Pastry

**Source:** ytower_cooking

Flaky store-bought pastry sheets filled with a seasoned shredded radish and scallion filling — a quick and satisfying savory snack.

---

## Ingredients

- 600g radish
- 1 tsp salt
- 40g scallion
- 1 tbsp shallot oil
- 1 tsp white pepper powder
- 6 store-bought pastry sheets
- Beaten eggs, q.s.
- White sesame, q.s.

---

## 作法

1. **Shred radish:** Peel the radish and shred it.

2. **Draw out moisture:** Add salt and mix well, then let sit for 5 minutes until water releases.

3. **Make filling:** Squeeze out excess water from the shredded radish, then add scallion, white pepper powder, and shallot oil. Mix to combine into the radish filling.

4. **Wrap:** Take a pastry sheet and fill with about 50g of filling. Fold and seal the edges, then lightly press into a flat disc shape.

5. **Prep for baking:** Brush the surface with beaten egg, coat with white sesame, place on a baking sheet, and rest for 15 minutes.

6. **Bake:** Place in a preheated oven and bake at 180°C for about 15 minutes.
