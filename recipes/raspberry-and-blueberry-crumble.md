# Raspberry and Blueberry Crumble

**Category:** Desserts

**Summary:** A classic British crumble from Delia Smith featuring raspberries and blueberries baked beneath a buttery, oaty crumble topping. Best served warm with cream, custard, or ice cream.

## Ingredients
- 225 g (8 oz) raspberries
- 225 g (8 oz) blueberries
- 50 g (2 oz) caster sugar
- 1 tsp cornflour

### For the crumble topping
- 110 g (4 oz) plain flour
- 75 g (3 oz) butter, chilled and cubed
- 75 g (3 oz) demerara sugar
- 50 g (2 oz) porridge oats

## Method
1. Preheat oven to 190°C / 375°F / Gas 5.
2. Toss the raspberries and blueberries with caster sugar and cornflour in a baking dish.
3. Rub the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
4. Stir in the demerara sugar and oats.
5. Sprinkle the crumble mixture evenly over the fruit.
6. Bake for 25–30 minutes, until the top is golden and the fruit is bubbling.
7. Serve warm with cream, custard, or ice cream.
