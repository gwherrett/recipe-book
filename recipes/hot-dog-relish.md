# Hot Dog Relish

**Yield:** 36 oz

A tangy, sweet-and-spiced green tomato relish made in the traditional pickled style — perfect for topping hot dogs and sausages.

---

## Ingredients

- 4 medium green tomatoes
- 2 large cucumbers, unpeeled
- 8 medium onions, peeled
- 1 small sweet red pepper, seeded
- ¼ cup pickling salt
- 1½ cups sugar
- 1¼ cups cider vinegar
- ¼ tsp. cayenne
- 2 tbsp. mixed pickling spices

---

## Instructions

1. **Chop vegetables:** Put all vegetables through the fine blade of the food chopper.

2. **Salt and rest:** Put in bowl, sprinkle with pickling salt and let stand overnight.

3. **Drain and rinse:** Drain well, then rinse and drain well again.

4. **Cook:** Put in large saucepan. Add sugar, vinegar and cayenne. Tie mixed pickling spices in a small cheesecloth bag and add. Bring to a boil, turn heat to medium and boil, uncovered, about 25 minutes or until thick.

5. **Jar:** Discard spice bag. Ladle into hot sterilized jars. Seal.
