# Horseradish Cream Sauce for Prime Rib

## Ingredients
- ½ cup sour cream  
- 2–3 tbsp prepared horseradish (adjust to taste)  
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard  
- 1 tsp white wine vinegar or lemon juice  
- ½ tsp salt  
- ¼ tsp black pepper  
- Optional: 1 tbsp mayonnaise for extra creaminess  
- Optional: 1 tsp chopped chives or parsley for garnish

## Instructions
1. In a small bowl, mix together sour cream, horseradish, mustard, vinegar/lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
2. Taste and adjust heat by adding more horseradish if desired.
3. (Optional) Stir in mayonnaise for a richer texture.
4. Chill for at least 30 minutes before serving to let the flavors meld.
5. Garnish with chopped herbs if using.

## Notes
This sauce pairs beautifully with prime rib. For a sharper kick, increase horseradish or use freshly grated horseradish root.
