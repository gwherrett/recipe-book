# Sherry Cake

A rich, moist cake made with sherry wine, using a Duncan Hines butter cake mix as the base. The sherry adds a sophisticated depth of flavor to this vintage dessert.

---

## Ingredients

- 1 Duncan Hines butter cake mix (golden cake)
- 4 eggs, beaten
- 3/4 cup oil
- 3/4 cup sherry (medium sweet sherry recommended)
- 1 package (3-4 oz) instant vanilla pudding mix
- 1-1/2 tsp nutmeg

---

## Instructions

1. **Mix the batter:**
   Put all ingredients in bowl except sherry. Beat until blended.

2. **Add sherry:**
   Add sherry and beat on high speed with mixer for 10 minutes.

3. **Bake:**
   Use angel cake pan (tube pan), greased and floured. Bake according to cake mix package directions (typically 350Â°F for 45-55 minutes, or until toothpick inserted comes out clean).

4. **Cool and serve:**
   Cool in pan for 10-15 minutes, then invert onto serving plate. Can be served plain or with whipped cream.

---

## Notes

- Vintage recipe from a handwritten recipe card
- The long beating time (10 minutes) helps incorporate air and create a tender crumb
- Medium sweet sherry works best; cream sherry or Amontillado are good choices
- Can substitute golden cake mix if butter cake mix is unavailable
- The nutmeg complements the sherry beautifully
- This cake is even better the next day after flavors have melded
