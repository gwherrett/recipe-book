# Mediterranean Egg White Muffins

**Source:** MedWeight Manual

**Cook Time:** 25–28 minutes
**Yield:** 12 muffins

High-protein, low-calorie egg white muffins packed with Mediterranean herbs and fresh vegetables — 41 calories and 7g of protein per muffin.

---

## Ingredients

- 2 cups egg whites
- 1 tsp dried basil
- ½ tsp dried oregano
- ½ tsp dried thyme
- Optional: garlic powder, black pepper to taste

### Filling

- ½ red or green bell pepper, diced
- 1 cup baby spinach, chopped
- ½ med carrot, shredded
- ¼ cup reduced-fat / light feta cheese, crumbled
- 3 scallions, diced (white and green parts)

---

## Directions

1. **Preheat oven:** Preheat oven to 375°F and coat a muffin tin with spray or fill cups with paraffin muffin cups and set aside.

2. **Mix egg whites:** Combine egg whites, basil, oregano and thyme in a medium bowl and whisk until frothy. Combine veggies in a large bowl and fill each muffin tin about ⅔ full.

3. **Fill tins:** Using a ¼ measuring cup, scoop egg white mixture and fill each muffin tin to the top. Top with feta.

4. **Bake:** Place in oven and cook for 25–28 mins or until slightly brown. Let cool for 5 mins before enjoying.

---

## Notes

- Other filling ideas: ham, sausage, bacon, mushrooms, tofu, zucchini, or anything else you have on hand!
- Nutrition per muffin: Calories 41, Protein 7g, Carbs 1g, Fat 1g
