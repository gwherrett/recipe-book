# Earthen Pot Fish Curry

**Source:** Earthen Pot Curry Powder packet

**Yield:** 4–6 servings

A Malaysian-style fish curry built around tamarind, curry powder, and aromatics. The brinjal and tomatoes add body, and coconut milk can be added optionally for a richer sauce.

---

## Ingredients

- 1 kg Fish Fillet
- 4 Tbsps (60ml) Vegetable Oil
- 2 tsps (4g) Fenugreek
- 2 Green Chillis (sliced into half)
- 1 Spring Curry Leaves (optional)
- 2 Onions (pounded finely)
- 6 Red Shallots (finely sliced)
- 1 Brinjal (quartered) (alternative: small Eggplant)
- 2 Tomatoes (sliced in half)
- 80g Earthen Pot Curry Powder, mixed with 2½ cups (625ml) water
- 2 Tbsps (75g) Tamarind pulp, mixed with 1 cup (250ml) water, then strained — use the liquid
- 1 Tbsp (30g) Coconut Cream Powder, mixed with ½ cup (125ml) water (or ½ cup coconut milk) — optional

---

## Method

1. **Fry aromatics:** Heat oil in a pot and add in fenugreek, green chilli, shallots and curry leaves. Stir till fragrant.

2. **Build the sauce:** Add in the curry powder mixture, tamarind juice, pounded onions and stir. Add 2 cups (500ml) water and salt to taste. Stir well.

3. **Add vegetables and fish:** Add in brinjal, tomatoes and lady finger, coconut milk and fish fillet. Cook another 10 minutes.

4. **Serve:** Remove from heat and serve hot.
