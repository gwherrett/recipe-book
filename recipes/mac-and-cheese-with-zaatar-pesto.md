# Mac & Cheese with Za'atar Pesto

**Source:** [Yotam Ottolenghi's Crowd Pleaser, Menu 1: The Full Roast Dinner](https://www.channel4.com/programmes/ottolenghis-crowd-pleasers)

**Prep Time:** 25 minutes
**Cook Time:** 55 minutes
**Yield:** 6 servings

A one-pan stovetop mac and cheese made with cavatappi pasta cooked directly in milk, finished with cheddar, feta, and cumin. Served with a vibrant za'atar and coriander pesto and an optional golden panko crust.

---

## Ingredients

### Mac & Cheese

- 300g dried cavatappi (or macaroni)
- 1200ml whole milk, plus 100ml extra
- 70g unsalted butter, cut into roughly 3cm pieces
- 3 garlic cloves, crushed
- ¼ tsp ground turmeric
- 390g frozen mixed vegetables (beans, peas, corn etc.), defrosted (optional)
- 1½ tsp cumin seeds, toasted and roughly crushed
- 75ml double cream
- 190g mature cheddar, roughly grated
- 150g feta, crumbled
- Fine sea salt and black pepper

### Za'atar Pesto

- 50g pine nuts, toasted
- 100ml olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
- 40g coriander
- 1 lemon, zested and 2 tbsp juice
- 2 tbsp za'atar
- ¼ tsp chilli flakes (optional)
- Fine sea salt and black pepper

### Crispy Top (optional)

- 70g panko breadcrumbs
- 30g cheddar, finely grated
- 1½ tbsp olive oil

---

## Instructions

1. **Cook the pasta:** Place a large sauté pan on medium-high heat with the pasta, milk, butter, garlic, turmeric, 1½ teaspoons of salt, and a good grind of black pepper. Bring to a simmer, then reduce the heat to medium. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 8–10 minutes until the pasta is nearly al dente. Mix in the vegetables (if using) and cook for 3 minutes more (or continue cooking for 3 minutes without them until al dente). Reduce the heat to low and stir in the cumin, cream, and cheddar until melted and the sauce has thickened. Stir in the feta.

2. **Crispy top (optional):** Preheat the grill to its highest setting. Mix the panko with the cheddar and oil and scatter over the pasta. Grill for 4–5 minutes, until golden and bubbling.

3. **Make the pesto:** Put all the pesto ingredients into the bowl of a food processor with ¼ teaspoon of salt and pulse until almost smooth. Transfer to a serving bowl.

4. **Serve:** Spoon some of the pesto over the pasta and serve the rest on the side.
