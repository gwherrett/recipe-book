# Tomatillo Jam

---

## Ingredients

- 1 pound tomatillos
- 1½ cups sugar
- 1 cup water
- 4 Tbsp fresh squeezed lime juice
- Rind of a lime
- Pinch of salt

---

## Directions

1. **Prep the tomatillos:** Remove husks from tomatillos, rinse and roughly chop.

2. **Prep the lime:** Grate the rind of the lime, then slice open to juice.

3. **Simmer:** Place all of the ingredients in a saucepan set over medium-high heat. Let the mixture come to a simmer and stir occasionally.

4. **Cook to jam consistency:** Cook until it has thickened and achieved a soft jam consistency. Do not over thicken as jam will thicken as it cools.
