# Insalata Capricciosa (Capricciosa Salad)

A creamy, savory Italian-style salad that blends shredded vegetables with cheese, ham, and a tangy dressing. Commonly served as an antipasto in Northern Italy.

---

## 🥗 Ingredients (Serves 4–6)

- **1½ cups** green cabbage, finely shredded  
- **1 cup** carrots, julienned or shredded  
- **1 cup** cooked ham, julienned or chopped  
- **¾ cup** Swiss cheese or provolone, julienned or cubed  
- **2 tbsp** pickles or gherkins, finely chopped *(optional but traditional)*  
- **2 tbsp** capers *(optional)*  
- **Salt & black pepper**, to taste  

### Dressing

- **5 tbsp** mayonnaise  
- **2 tsp** Dijon or mild mustard  
- **1 tbsp** white wine vinegar or lemon juice  
- **1–2 tbsp** whole milk or plain yogurt (to thin, optional)  

---

## 🥄 Instructions

1. **Prep the ingredients:**  
   In a large bowl, combine the shredded cabbage, carrots, ham, cheese, pickles, and capers.

2. **Make the dressing:**  
   In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, mustard, and vinegar or lemon juice. Add milk/yogurt if you'd like it a bit lighter or thinner. Season with salt and pepper.

3. **Combine:**  
   Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well to coat everything evenly.

4. **Chill & serve:**  
   Let the salad rest in the fridge for at least 30 minutes to meld flavors. Serve cold, optionally garnished with fresh parsley or a few extra pickle slices.

---

## 🔄 Variations

- Substitute **turkey or chicken** for ham  
- Use **celeriac** or **bell pepper** for more crunch  
- Add a few **olives** or **artichoke hearts** for flair
