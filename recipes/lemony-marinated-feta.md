# Lemony Marinated Feta

**Source:** [Yotam Ottolenghi's Crowd Pleaser, Menu 2: The Weeknight Dinner](https://www.channel4.com/programmes/ottolenghis-crowd-pleasers)

**Prep Time:** 5 minutes
**Cook Time:** 5 minutes
**Marinating Time:** 30 minutes or overnight
**Yield:** 6 servings

Cubed feta infused in warm olive oil fragrant with lemon zest, caraway seeds, chilli flakes, and dried oregano. Ready in minutes and even better made ahead — serve as a mezze, on toast, or alongside any Middle Eastern spread.

---

## Ingredients

- 60ml olive oil
- 1 lemon, 7 strips of zest removed with a peeler
- 1 tsp caraway seeds, lightly crushed in a pestle and mortar
- ½ tsp chilli flakes
- ½ tsp dried oregano
- 200g feta, cut into 1½cm pieces

---

## Instructions

1. **Infuse the oil:** Place the olive oil in a small pan over medium heat. Once hot, add the lemon zest, caraway seeds, chilli flakes, and oregano. Warm for 2 minutes until fragrant.

2. **Marinate:** Place the feta in a heatproof bowl and pour over the hot oil. Set aside to infuse for at least 30 minutes, then transfer to a jar and refrigerate.

---

## Notes

- The feta keeps refrigerated for up to 1 week. The oil solidifies when cold — let it come to room temperature before serving.
- Serve as part of a mezze, on grilled bread, or alongside rice dishes and braises.
