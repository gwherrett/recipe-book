# Traditional Lebanese Cabbage Salad

**Source:** Jamjar

**Yield:** 4–6 servings

Cabbage salad is the traditional accompaniment for Mujadara. It is often served on menus by restaurants for more popular salads like fattoush or tabour, however it is a staple of most Lebanese homes and therefore in line with Jamjar's approach of offering home style folk food.

---

## Ingredients

### Dressing

- ¼ yellow onion
- ¼ tomato
- ¼ cucumber
- 2 cloves garlic
- ½ cup fresh mint
- 250ml olive oil
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 2 tsp salt

### Cabbage Salad

- 1 medium green cabbage
- ½ red cabbage
- 2 tbsp dried mint
- 1 cup parsley

---

## Instructions

### Dressing

1. **Blend:** Place all ingredients in a blender and blend until fully emulsified.
2. **Store:** Transfer to a sealed container and refrigerate for up to a week. Shake well before use.

### Cabbage Salad

1. **Shred the cabbage:** Finely shred the green and red cabbage.
2. **Chop the parsley:** Chop the parsley finely.
3. **Toss:** Toss the cabbage, parsley and dry mint with a good portion of the dressing until well mixed.
