# Simple Syrup

A liquid sweetener made by dissolving sugar in water. Perfect for cocktails, coffee, tea, and desserts.

## Basic Simple Syrup Recipe

### Ingredients:
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup water

### Instructions:
1. Combine sugar and water in a saucepan over medium heat.
2. Stir until the sugar is completely dissolved.
3. Remove from heat and let it cool.
4. Store in a sealed container in the refrigerator for up to a month.

## Variations:

- **Rich Simple Syrup:** Use a 2:1 ratio of sugar to water for a thicker syrup.
- **Flavored Simple Syrup:** Infuse with ingredients like vanilla, mint, cinnamon, or citrus zest while heating, then strain before storing.
