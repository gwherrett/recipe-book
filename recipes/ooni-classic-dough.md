# Ooni Classic Dough

**Source:** Ooni Top 10 Recipes eBook

**Cook Time:** 60 seconds (per pizza)
**Yield:** 4x 12" pizzas

---

## Ingredients

- 368g lukewarm water
- 18g salt
- 3.5g active dry yeast (or 2.6g instant dried yeast)
- 613g "00" flour, plus extra for dusting

---

## Instructions

1. **Mix yeast and flour:** Fill a bowl with lukewarm water and add the active dry yeast, using a whisk to mix. In a separate bowl, mix the flour and salt together, then pour into the water.

2. **Knead and bulk rise:** Stir with a wooden spoon until a dough starts to form and it comes together in a ball. Place onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 10 minutes, until firm and stretchy. Return to the bowl. Cover with a towel and let rise in a warm place for 2 hours, or until doubled in size.

3. **Divide and second rise:** When the dough is ready, divide into 3 or 4 equal pieces. Place each in a separate bowl or tray, cover with a dish towel and let rise in a warm place for 30 to 60 minutes, or until doubled in size.

4. **Preheat oven:** Fire up your oven and allow it to preheat for 20 minutes. Aim for between 450°C and 500°C on your baking stone. Check this safely and accurately with an infrared thermometer.

5. **Shape the base:** Place your dough ball on a lightly floured surface and use your fingertips to press it into a small, flat disc. From the center, push the dough outwards while spreading your fingers, making the disc bigger. Pick up the dough and pinch around the edge, allowing gravity to pull it downwards into a circle.

6. **Top and launch:** Lightly flour your peel and lay the base on it, then add your toppings.

7. **Cook:** Launch your pizza and cook for 1 to 2 minutes, turning every 20 seconds for an even bake.
