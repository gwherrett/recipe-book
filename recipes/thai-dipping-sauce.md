# Thai Dipping Sauce

A classic Thai dipping sauce that's perfect for grilled meats, spring rolls, or seafood.

## Ingredients
- 1/4 cup fish sauce  
- 1/4 cup lime juice  
- 1/4 cup water  
- 2 tablespoons sugar  
- 2 cloves garlic, minced  
- 1â€“2 Thai chilies, finely chopped (adjust to your heat preference)  
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped cilantro  
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped mint (optional)  
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped green onion  
- 1 teaspoon rice vinegar (optional)

## Instructions
1. **Combine Ingredients**: In a small bowl, combine the fish sauce, lime juice, water, and sugar. Stir until the sugar is fully dissolved.
2. **Add Aromatics**: Add the minced garlic, chopped Thai chilies, cilantro, mint (if using), and green onion to the bowl. Mix well.
3. **Adjust Flavor**: Taste the sauce and adjust the flavors as needed. You can add more lime juice for acidity, more sugar for sweetness, or more chilies for heat.
4. **Serve**: Let the sauce sit for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Serve it as a dipping sauce for your favorite dishes.

## Notes
- Store leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
