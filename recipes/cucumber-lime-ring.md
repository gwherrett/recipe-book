# Cucumber-Lime Ring

**Yield:** Serves 6

A classic chilled gelatin mould made with lime jelly, freshly grated cucumber, sour cream, and mayonnaise, studded with radish slices and served on lettuce with cucumber and radish garnish.

---

## Ingredients

### Ring

- 3-oz. pkg. lime gelatin
- 1½ tsp. salt
- 1 cup boiling water
- 2 small cucumbers OR 1 very large
- 3 tbsp. white vinegar
- 1½ tsp. grated onion
- ½ cup commercial sour cream
- ¼ cup thick mayonnaise
- ¼ cup paper-thin radish slices

### Garnish

- Lettuce
- Sliced cucumbers
- Sliced radishes

---

## Instructions

1. **Dissolve gelatin:** Combine gelatin and salt and add boiling water. Stir until gelatin is dissolved.

2. **Prepare cucumber:** Peel cucumbers and grate on fine grater into a bowl. Drain well, saving liquid. Measure out 1½ cups of grated cucumber pulp and ¼ cup of the liquid.

3. **Combine:** Add the cucumber liquid to the gelatin mixture along with the vinegar and onion. Chill until beginning to set.

4. **Finish and mould:** Add sour cream and mayonnaise and beat well with rotary beater. Stir in grated cucumber and ¼ cup radish slices. Pour into 9-inch ring mould and chill until firm.

5. **Serve:** Unmould on lettuce and garnish with sliced cucumbers and radishes.
