# Cheese Crackers

Homemade cheese crackers with sharp cheddar, Parmesan, and walnuts. These savory crackers are perfect for entertaining or snacking.

**Yield:** About 8 dozen 2-inch shapes

---

## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 2 cups grated old cheddar
- ½ cup grated Parmesan
- 1 cup flour
- ¼ tsp cayenne
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 egg, beaten
- ½ cup finely chopped walnuts

---

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven:**
   Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).

2. **Cream butter and cheeses:**
   Cream butter, then stir in grated cheeses.

3. **Add dry ingredients:**
   In separate bowl, mix flour, cayenne and salt. Add cheese mixture and mix well.

4. **Knead and shape:**
   Knead mixture a few times on lightly floured board. Divide in 2. Roll out one piece at a time to about ⅛ inch thick.

5. **Cut and top:**
   Cut out shapes. Brush each shape with egg, sprinkle with walnuts.

6. **Bake:**
   Bake for 8 minutes or until golden brown.

---

## Notes

- Makes about 8 dozen 2-inch shapes
- Can use cookie cutters in any shape - stars, circles, squares
- These freeze well - store in airtight container with parchment between layers
- Can be made ahead and frozen unbaked, then baked from frozen (add 1-2 minutes)
- The cayenne adds a subtle kick - adjust to taste
- Use old (aged) cheddar for best flavor
- Vintage handwritten recipe
