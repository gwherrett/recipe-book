# Simple Chickpea Salad

**Category:** Salads & Bowls  
**Tags:** Vegetarian, Quick, Protein-Rich, Meal Prep  

**Summary:**  
A fresh, colorful chickpea salad with roasted red peppers, feta, basil, and a light lemon-garlic dressing. Perfect as a side dish or a satisfying vegetarian lunch. Adaptable with seasonal vegetables and herbs.  

## Ingredients  
- 2 cans (about 3 cups) chickpeas, rinsed and drained  
- 1 cup roasted red peppers, chopped  
- ½ cup feta cheese, crumbled  
- ½ cup fresh basil, chopped  
- ¼ cup red onion, finely diced  
- 2 cloves garlic, minced  
- Zest and juice of 1 lemon  
- 3 tbsp olive oil  
- Salt and pepper, to taste  

## Method  
1. In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, roasted red peppers, feta, basil, and onion.  
2. In a small bowl, whisk together garlic, lemon zest and juice, and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper.  
3. Pour dressing over salad and toss to coat evenly.  
4. Chill for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow flavors to meld.  

**Keeps:** Up to 3 days refrigerated.
