#### **Method**
1. If using fresh lima beans, boil in salted water until tender, then drain.  
2. Heat butter or oil in a skillet over medium heat.  
3. SautÃ© onion until translucent, then add garlic and cook 1 minute.  
4. Add red bell pepper (if using) and cook until softened.  
5. Stir in corn and lima beans; cook until heated through.  
6. (Optional) Add cream and simmer until lightly thickened.  
7. Season with salt and pepper.  
8. Stir in herbs just before serving.
