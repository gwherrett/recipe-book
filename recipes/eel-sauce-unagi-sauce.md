# Eel Sauce (Unagi Sauce)

## Ingredients

### Version 1
- 1 cup soy sauce
- 1 cup mirin
- 4 Tbsp sake

### Version 2
- 1/2 cup soy sauce
- 1/2 cup mirin
- 1/4 cup sugar

## Method
1. Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan.
2. Bring to a gentle simmer over medium heat.
3. Reduce until slightly thickened, about 10â€“15 minutes.
4. Cool and store in an airtight container in the refrigerator.
