# Chicken and Chorizo Paella

**Yield:** 4 servings

---

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon oil
- 14 ounces chorizo, sliced on the diagonal
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 1 red bell pepper, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, chopped
- ¼ teaspoon paprika
- 2 cups white rice (long grain or short grain)
- 1 pinch saffron
- 2 bay leaves
- ½ cup white wine
- 3½ cups chicken stock
- 1 chicken, cut in 8 pieces, bone in
- ½ cup peas, frozen
- 2 tomatoes, skinned, seeded and chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste

---

## Instructions

1. **Brown the chicken:** Heat oven to 400°F. Heat oil in a large casserole or paella pan over high heat. Brown chicken pieces and remove. Transfer chicken pieces to an ovenproof dish and bake for 20 minutes.

2. **Cook the aromatics:** To the large casserole or paella pan add chorizo and cook until browned and fragrant, about 1 minute. Add onion, red bell pepper, and garlic. Cook, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are tender, about 3 minutes.

3. **Add rice and liquids:** Add rice, paprika, saffron, and bay leaves. Stir to combine and coat the rice, about 1 minute. Add white wine and cook until absorbed. Add chicken stock and bring to a boil. Add brown chicken pieces, frozen peas, and tomato; stir to combine.

4. **Bake:** Transfer skillet to oven. Cook uncovered until rice is tender and no liquid remains, about 25 minutes.

5. **Serve:** Remove from the oven. Fluff with a fork and serve immediately.
