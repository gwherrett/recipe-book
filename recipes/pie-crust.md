# Pie Crust

## Ingredients
- 4 cups flour  
- 1 tsp salt  
- 1 tbsp sugar  
- 1 tsp baking powder  
- 1 3/4 cups shortening *(or 1 cup shortening + 3/4 cup butter)*  
- 1 egg  
- 1/2 cup water  
- 1 tbsp vinegar  

## Directions
1. Combine flour, salt, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.  
2. Cut shortening into dry ingredients using a pastry cutter.  
3. In a cup, whisk egg, water, and vinegar together.  
4. Add liquid mixture to the flour/shortening mixture. Mix with a fork, then gently with hands. *Do not over‑knead.*  
5. Form into a ball, wrap in wax paper, and refrigerate for 1 hour.  
6. Divide dough into four portions. Makes two 9-inch pie shells.  
7. For baking:  
   - Poke holes in bottom crust.  
   - For top crust, pinch edges and poke holes.  
8. Bake at **425°F for 10 minutes**, then reduce to **350°F** to finish.
