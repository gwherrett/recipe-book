# Lo Bak Go (Turnip Cake)

**Yield:** 1 large cake (serves 6–8)

A classic Cantonese dim sum staple made from shredded lo bak (white radish/carrot) and rice flour, steamed with savoury fillings until firm and sliceable.

---

## Ingredients

- 4–5 lb lo bak (white carrot / daikon radish), shredded
- 400g rice flour
- 4 tablespoons cornstarch
- 4½ cups water (2½ cups for cooking the lo bak, 2 cups for the flour mixture)

### Filling

- Dried shrimp
- Chinese sausage, diced
- Dried mushrooms, pre-soaked and diced
- Light soy sauce
- Cooking wine
- White pepper
- Chicken stock powder

### Topping

- Green onion, chopped
- Sesame seeds

---

## Instructions

1. **Cook the lo bak:** Use 2½ cups water to cook the shredded lo bak over medium heat until softened. Turn off the heat.

2. **Mix the flour:** Combine the rice flour and cornstarch with 2 cups water and mix until smooth.

3. **Stir-fry the filling:** Stir-fry the dried shrimp, diced Chinese sausage, and pre-soaked diced mushrooms together. Season with light soy sauce, cooking wine, white pepper, and chicken stock powder.

4. **Combine:** Pour the rice flour mixture into the hot lo bak mixture and stir to combine. Add the stir-fried filling ingredients and mix well.

5. **Steam:** Lightly oil a cake pan. Pour the mixture in. Steam over boiling water for approximately 30–40 minutes. Test doneness by inserting a toothpick — if it comes out clean with no sticky batter, it is done.

6. **Finish:** Sprinkle chopped green onion and sesame seeds on top.
