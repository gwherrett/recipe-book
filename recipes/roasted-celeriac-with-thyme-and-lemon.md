# Roasted Celeriac with Thyme and Lemon

**Source:** [Yotam Ottolenghi's Crowd Pleaser, Menu 1: The Full Roast Dinner](https://www.channel4.com/programmes/ottolenghis-crowd-pleasers)

**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 1 hour 45 minutes
**Yield:** 4–6 servings

Whole celeriac slow-roasted until meltingly tender, then bathed in fragrant olive oil with thyme and lemon zest. Served in wedges with crème fraîche.

---

## Ingredients

- 2 celeriac (1.2kg), roots trimmed
- 150ml olive oil
- 10g thyme sprigs
- 1 lemon, zest removed in strips with a peeler
- Flaked sea salt and black pepper

### To Serve

- Crème fraîche
- Extra salt
- Lemon halves

---

## Instructions

1. **Preheat the oven** to 160°C.

2. **First roast:** Place the celeriac in a high-sided tray. Pour over 2 tablespoons of oil and rub over 2 teaspoons of salt and a good grind of pepper. Roast for 1 hour 30 minutes, until a knife goes through very easily.

3. **Infuse:** Pour in the remaining olive oil, thyme, and lemon zest. Return to the oven and cook for another 10 minutes.

4. **Rest and slice:** Set aside for 5 minutes, then slice each celeriac into 6 wedges. Place into the tray and spoon over the oil.

5. **Serve:** Sprinkle with salt and serve with lemon halves to squeeze on top and crème fraîche alongside.
