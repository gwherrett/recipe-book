# Extra Stuffings for Zucchini Flowers

These stuffing mixtures will fill **approximately 20 zucchini flowers**. Each mixture can be used independently.

## 1. Breadcrumbs, Anchovies & Parsley

### Ingredients
- 1 cup coarse breadcrumbs
- 8–10 anchovy fillets, finely chopped
- 3 tbsp finely chopped Italian parsley
- Grated rind of ½ lemon
- Salt and ground black pepper, to taste

### Method
1. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl with a fork until well combined.
2. Pack into each zucchini flower using a teaspoon.

## 2. Ricotta & Herbs

### Ingredients
- 200 g ricotta cheese
- 2 tbsp snipped chives
- 2 tbsp chopped Italian parsley

### Method
1. Mix ingredients together with a fork until smooth.
2. Stuff each zucchini flower with a teaspoonful of the mixture.

## 3. Minced Prawn

### Ingredients
- 250 g raw prawn meat
- Grated rind of ½ lemon
- 1 egg white, lightly beaten
- Salt and pepper, to taste

### Method
1. Combine all ingredients in a food processor.
2. Blend to a smooth paste.
3. Use the paste to stuff the zucchini flowers.

## 4. Spinach & Feta

### Ingredients
- 100 g thawed frozen spinach (or 100 g cooked spinach)
- 100 g sheep’s milk feta, crumbled
- Ground black pepper
- Good pinch of nutmeg

### Method
1. Break up the spinach in a bowl.
2. Add the crumbled feta, pepper, and nutmeg.
3. Mix well (do not add salt—the feta is salty enough).
4. Use to fill each zucchini flower.
