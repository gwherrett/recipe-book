# White Bean Dip

---

## Ingredients

- 1 (15-ounce) can white beans
- 2 cloves garlic
- 2 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
- ⅓ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup (loosely packed) fresh parsley leaves
- Salt and ground black pepper to taste

---

## Directions

1. **Drain the beans:** Drain and rinse the beans.

2. **Combine:** Place the beans, garlic, lemon juice, ⅓ cup olive oil, and parsley in a food processor.

3. **Season:** Season with salt and pepper.

4. **Blend:** Pulse until the mixture is coarsely chopped, adding water as needed to reach a puree.

5. **Finish and serve:** Taste and adjust the seasoning. Serve drizzled with olive oil and sprinkled with a bit of cumin or paprika and some parsley.
