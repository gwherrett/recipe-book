# Turkey Tetrazzini (Classic)

**Yield:** 3 × 8" square pans (freeze-ahead)

A rich, freeze-ahead tetrazzini made with a cream-and-egg-yolk sauce, sherry, and a full pound of spaghetti — designed to be made in bulk and reheated straight from frozen.

---

## Ingredients

- ¾ cup butter or margarine
- ¾ cup all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 drops hot pepper sauce
- Freshly ground pepper to taste
- 3 cups milk
- 2 cups turkey or chicken broth
- 4 egg yolks
- ½ cup heavy cream
- ¼ cup sherry
- 1 jar (4½ ounces) whole mushrooms, drained
- 6 cups diced cooked turkey
- 1 package (1 pound) thin spaghetti
- 4 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
- Paprika

---

## Instructions

1. **Make sauce:** Melt butter in Dutch oven or kettle. Gradually stir in flour to make a smooth paste. Add salt, hot pepper sauce and pepper and mix well. Gradually stir in milk and broth. Bring to boil over medium heat and cook, stirring, until smooth and slightly thickened.

2. **Add egg yolks:** Beat egg yolks with cream. Add a small amount of hot sauce and blend. Gradually add to hot mixture and cook, stirring, over low heat until heated.

3. **Add turkey:** Add sherry, mushrooms and turkey and remove from heat.

4. **Cook pasta:** Cook spaghetti until barely tender; drain and add to first mixture.

5. **Assemble and freeze:** Divide among three 8" square foil freezer pans. Sprinkle with cheese, then dust with paprika. Cover with waxed paper, then with heavy foil and freeze.

6. **Reheat:** Bake in unpreheated 350° oven until bubbly.
