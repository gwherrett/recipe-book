# Black Chickpeas with Baharat and Orange

**Source:** [Yotam Ottolenghi's Crowd Pleaser, Menu 2: The Weeknight Dinner](https://www.channel4.com/programmes/ottolenghis-crowd-pleasers)

**Prep Time:** 5 minutes
**Cook Time:** 1 hour 30 minutes
**Yield:** 4 servings

A deeply flavoured, low-effort braise of chickpeas with cherry tomatoes, whole garlic cloves, sliced orange, and green olives, spiced with baharat and turmeric. Finished with fresh tarragon and served with Greek yoghurt.

---

## Ingredients

- 1 jar black or regular chickpeas (or black beans), drained (400g)
- 250g cherry tomatoes, red and yellow
- 12 garlic cloves, peeled and smashed with the flat side of a knife
- 1 small orange, unpeeled, quartered and thinly sliced (100g)
- 50g pitted green olives
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 tbsp baharat
- ½ tsp turmeric
- 100ml olive oil
- 5g tarragon leaves
- Greek yoghurt, to serve
- Fine sea salt

---

## Instructions

1. **Braise:** Add everything except the tarragon and yoghurt to a 26cm sauté pan, along with ½ teaspoon of salt and 100ml of water. Gently mix to combine and place on medium-high heat. Once simmering, cover, reduce the heat to low, and cook gently for 1½ hours, stirring halfway through.

2. **Rest:** Remove from the heat and set aside to rest for 10 minutes.

3. **Finish and serve:** Stir in the tarragon and serve with a dollop of yoghurt alongside.
