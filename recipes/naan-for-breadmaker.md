# Naan for Breadmaker

**Category:** Breads / Indian
**Servings:** 6 (makes ~12 small naan)
**Prep Time:** 20 minutes  
**Cook Time:** 10–15 minutes  
**Total Time:** ~30 minutes

---

## Ingredients
- **1/2 tbsp** active dry yeast
- **3 cups** all-purpose flour
- **1/2 tbsp** sugar *(assumed – original text missing label)*
- **1/2 tsp** salt
- **2 tbsp** oil *(assumed – original text missing label)*
- **3/4 cup** plain yogurt
- **1/2 cup** water
- **4 tbsp** melted butter (for brushing)
- **4 tbsp** finely chopped onions (optional topping)

---

## Directions
1. Add all ingredients **except melted butter and onions** to your bread machine.  
   Use the **Dough Cycle**.

2. When complete, remove the dough and divide into **12 equal portions**.

3. Preheat oven to **500°F (260°C)**.  
   Ensure it is *fully* preheated.

4. Roll each piece of dough into a circle about **8 inches** in diameter.

5. Place on a greased pizza pan or baking sheet.

6. Brush the top of each naan with **melted butter**, then press **finely chopped onions** into the surface.

7. Bake for **10–15 minutes**, until puffy and golden brown.

---

## Notes
- You may omit the onions or replace with garlic.
- Dough can also be cooked in a cast‑iron skillet for a more traditional finish.
- For softer naan, cover with a towel immediately after baking.
