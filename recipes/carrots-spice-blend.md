## Variations
- **Middle Eastern:** Add 1/2 tsp cumin + 1/2 tsp coriander + drizzle of tahini before serving.  
- **Moroccan:** Add 1/4 tsp each of cinnamon, ginger, and turmeric.  
- **Indian:** Add 1/2 tsp garam masala + pinch of turmeric.  
- **Herbaceous:** Toss with fresh thyme or rosemary before roasting.  
- **Chili-Lime:** Mix with chipotle powder and finish with lime zest and juice.
