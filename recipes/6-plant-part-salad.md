# 6 Plant Part Salad with Fresh Roots Famous Dressing

**Source:** [Fresh Roots](https://freshroots.ca)

A garden-to-table salad built from all six edible plant parts — roots, stems, leaves, flowers, fruits, and seeds — dressed with a tangy nutritional yeast vinaigrette.

---

## Ingredients

### For the Salad

- **Roots:** Carrots
- **Stems:** Stem part of swiss chard
- **Leaves:** Lettuce, leaf part of swiss chard
- **Flowers:** Nasturtiums, calendula petals, or kale flowers
- **Fruits:** Zucchini and cucumbers are technically fruits!
- **Seeds:** Sunflower seeds, pumpkin seeds or fresh peas

### For the Dressing

- ¾ cup nutritional yeast
- ⅓ cup water
- ⅓ cup soy sauce or tamari
- ⅓ cup apple cider vinegar
- 2 large cloves garlic, pressed or very finely chopped
- ½ cup sunflower or mildly-flavoured olive oil

---

## Directions

1. **Make the dressing:** Add all dressing ingredients except the oil to a mason jar and shake well to combine. Add oil, and shake some more until incorporated.

2. **Assemble the salad:** Harvest, wash and tear or cut the vegetables and toss with dressing. Save the colourful Swiss Chard stems to cut to make into "veggie confetti," and use that and the flowers as garnish.
