# Wild Mushroom Risotto

**Yield:** 4 servings

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup Arborio rice
- ¼ cup wild rice
- 3 (14½ oz) cans chicken broth
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 shallots, chopped
- 1 pound wild mushrooms
- ½ cup white wine
- ½ cup Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
- ¾ teaspoon fresh thyme, chopped
- ½ teaspoon truffle oil

---

## Instructions

1. **Heat the broth:** Bring chicken broth to a simmer and keep hot.

2. **Sauté mushrooms:** Melt 3 tablespoons butter with olive oil and sauté mushrooms until all liquid is evaporated.

3. **Build the risotto:** Add rice and wild rice and stir to coat. Add wine and simmer until liquid is absorbed, stirring frequently. Increase heat to medium-high. Add ¾ cup hot broth and simmer until absorbed, stirring frequently. Add remaining hot broth ¾ cup at a time, allowing broth to be absorbed before adding more and stirring frequently until rice is just tender and mixture is creamy.

4. **Finish:** Stir in mushroom, Parmesan cheese, truffle oil, and chopped fresh
