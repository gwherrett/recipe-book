# Pesto–Ricotta Spread / Dip

**Category:** Spreads & Dips  
**Serves:** 4  
**Tags:** Italian, Appetizer, Vegetarian  

---

### Summary  
A creamy, savory blend of ricotta and pesto that’s perfect as a spread or dip. The ricotta balances pesto’s bold, herby flavor with a smooth, lightly sweet texture. Serve with crostini, crackers, or fresh vegetables.

---

### Ingredients  
- 200 g ricotta cheese (about ¾ cup)  
- 45 ml pesto (3 tbsp)  
- 10 ml olive oil (2 tsp, optional)  
- Pinch of salt & freshly ground black pepper  
- ½ tsp lemon zest or 1 tsp lemon juice (optional)

---

### Method  
1. Whip ricotta in a bowl until smooth.  
2. Stir in pesto and olive oil.  
3. Taste and season with salt, pepper, and lemon zest or juice.  
4. Chill 15–20 minutes before serving.

---

### Serving Suggestions  
- Spread on crostini or crackers.  
- Serve as a dip with vegetables.  
- Spoon over grilled chicken or zucchini.  
- Garnish with toasted pine nuts or a drizzle of olive oil for presentation.
