# Coriander Roast Potatoes

**Source:** Fırın

**Yield:** 4–6 servings

Crispy roast potatoes lifted with lemon, garlic, coriander, and fresh cilantro — a Cypriot take on the classic known as *Golyandrolu Patates*.

---

## Ingredients

- 3 lb 5 oz (1.5 kg) Cyprus or Yukon Gold potatoes
- 2 tsp fresh lemon juice
- 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp sea salt flakes
- ½ tsp cracked black pepper
- 1 tsp coriander seeds
- ¼ tsp garlic, crushed
- 1 tbsp finely grated lemon zest
- 2 tsp dried oregano
- 3 tbsp finely chopped fresh cilantro leaves

---

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven:** Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).

2. **Parboil and rough up the potatoes:** Peel, wash, and cut the potatoes into quarters or chunky wedges, place them in a large pot, and cover with cold water. Place the pot over high heat, bring to a boil, then reduce the heat a little and simmer the potatoes for 6 minutes. Remove the pot from the heat and drain the potatoes very well in a colander over a sink. Wipe out the pot with paper towels to ensure it is completely dry, then return the potatoes to the pot and sprinkle over the lemon juice and flour. Put the lid on, holding it closed shut, and give the pot a good shake to rough up the edges of the potatoes. Carefully transfer the potatoes back to the colander and let them steam dry for 15–20 minutes.

3. **Roast the potatoes:** Pour the olive oil into a large, nonstick roasting pan and place it on the middle rack of the oven to heat. After around 15 minutes, once the oil is smoking, remove the pan from the oven and lay it somewhere flat and heatproof. Carefully transfer the potatoes straight into the hot oil and gently tilt the pan and spoon some of the hot oil over the top of the potatoes. Ensure that every potato is fully coated with oil, sprinkle with half of the sea salt and all of the black pepper, then return the potatoes to the oven. Roast for 20–25 minutes, then remove from the oven, turn them over, and return them to the oven for another 20–25 minutes while you prepare the coating.

4. **Prepare the coating:** Lightly crush the coriander seeds with a mortar and pestle then pour half of them into a large, deep dish that will be big enough to hold all the potatoes. Add the crushed garlic, lemon zest, and dried oregano and give everything a good stir.

5. **Coat and finish:** Remove the pan from the oven and, using a slotted spoon or spatula, transfer all of the potatoes to the large dish with the coriander seed mixture and give the potatoes a really good stir so that they are fully coated. Then quickly return them to the hot roasting pan and give them a further 5 minutes in the oven before serving. Take the pan out of the oven, add the fresh cilantro and remaining salt, gently tossing the potatoes in the herbs and seasoning, then transfer everything to a serving platter.
