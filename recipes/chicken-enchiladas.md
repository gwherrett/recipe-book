# Enchiladas with Chipotle Shredded Chicken or Turkey

Red & green enchiladas with a chipotle-spiced pressure-cooked filling. Works equally well with chicken legs or turkey drumsticks.

---

## Ingredients

### Protein
- **1.3–1.6 kg bone-in, skin-on chicken legs** (thighs/drumsticks), skin removed
  *or* **1.3–1.6 kg turkey drumsticks**, skin removed
- 1 tbsp neutral oil (optional, for sauté)
- 1 medium onion, finely chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 jalapeño or serrano, minced (optional)
- 400 g can diced tomatoes (14 oz), with juices
- 240 ml low-sodium chicken or turkey stock (1 cup)
- 1–2 tbsp adobo from canned chipotles, or 1 chipotle in adobo, minced
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar or lime juice

### Spice Mix
- 2 tsp ground cumin
- 2 tsp dried oregano (Mexican if possible)
- 1½ tsp smoked paprika (or 1 tsp regular + ½ tsp ancho powder)
- 1 tsp ground coriander (optional)
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp kosher salt (½ tsp if table salt)
- ½ tsp black pepper
- 1 bay leaf

### Sauces
- 2 cups prepared tomatillo / green enchilada sauce
- 1 batch **[Salsa Roja](salsa-roja.md)**

### Assembly
- 8–12 corn tortillas
- 2 cups shredded cheese (Monterey Jack, Oaxaca, or Jack/Cheddar mix)
- Neutral oil for softening tortillas

### Toppings
- Cilantro, sliced avocado, lime wedges, thinly sliced red onion, Mexican crema or sour cream

---

## Method

### 1. Season the Protein
Pat meat dry. Toss with spice mix (reserve the bay leaf).

### 2. Sauté Aromatics *(optional but adds depth)*
On *Sauté* (Normal), heat oil. Cook onion 3–4 min until translucent; add garlic and jalapeño 30–60 sec until fragrant.

### 3. Pressure Cook
Add tomatoes, stock, chipotle/adobo, tomato paste, and vinegar/lime. Stir to combine, then nestle in the protein and bay leaf.

| Protein | High Pressure | Natural Release |
|---|---|---|
| Chicken legs | 15 min | 10 min |
| Turkey drumsticks | 25 min | 15 min |

Quick-release any remaining pressure after natural release.

### 4. Shred & Reduce
Transfer meat to a tray; discard bones. Shred with two forks. Switch pot to *Sauté* and simmer sauce 5–8 min to thicken. Return shredded meat and toss to coat. Adjust salt and lime to taste.

### 5. Make the Salsa Roja
Follow the [Salsa Roja](salsa-roja.md) recipe. Can be made up to a week ahead.

### 6. Prep Tortillas
Lightly pan-fry each tortilla in a film of oil 10–15 sec per side until pliable. Alternatively, steam or microwave wrapped in a damp towel.

### 7. Assemble
1. Preheat oven to **375°F / 190°C**.
2. Divide a baking dish visually in half. Spoon a thin layer of red sauce on one side, green on the other.
3. Dip each tortilla in its sauce, fill with shredded meat and a little cheese, roll seam-side down into the matching half of the dish.
4. Spoon remaining red over red half, green over green half. Top with remaining cheese.

### 8. Bake
Bake **18–22 min** until bubbling and lightly golden at the edges.

### 9. Serve
Rest 5 minutes. Top with cilantro, crema, onion, avocado, and lime.

---

## Notes & Variations
- **Extra juicy filling:** mix ¼–½ cup of sauce into the shredded meat before filling.
- **Turkey tip:** turkey drumstick meat is denser and slightly gamier — the chipotle and lime brighten it well. Shred while still very warm.
- **Heat level:** add extra chipotle, minced jalapeño, or a few dashes of hot sauce.
- **Make-ahead:** assemble, cover, and refrigerate up to 24 hours; bake 5–10 min longer from cold.
- **Gluten-free:** use 100% corn tortillas; verify chili powder and tomato sauce labels.
- **Leftover filling:** use in tacos, tostadas, burritos, quesadillas, or chilaquiles.

## Storage
Refrigerate up to 4 days or freeze (filling only) up to 3 months. Reheat enchiladas covered at 350°F until warmed through.
