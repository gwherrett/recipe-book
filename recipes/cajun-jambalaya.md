# Cajun Jambalaya

**Source:** Emeril Lagasse (adapted)

## Ingredients

- 12 medium shrimp, peeled, deveined, chopped  
- 4 oz chicken, diced  
- 1 Tbsp Creole seasoning (see recipe below)  
- 2 Tbsp olive oil  
- 1/4 cup onion, chopped  
- 1/4 cup green bell pepper, chopped  
- 1/4 cup celery, chopped  
- 2 Tbsp garlic, chopped  
- 1/2 cup tomatoes, chopped  
- 3 bay leaves  
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce  
- 1 tsp hot sauce  
- 3/4 cup rice  
- 3 cups chicken stock  
- 5 oz Andouille sausage, sliced  
- Salt & pepper, to taste  

## Method

1. Toss shrimp and chicken with the Creole seasoning; rub well to coat.  
2. Heat oil in a large saucepan over high heat. Add onion, pepper, and celery; cook 3 minutes.  
3. Add garlic, tomatoes, bay leaves, Worcestershire, and hot sauce.  
4. Stir in rice, then slowly add chicken stock.  
5. Reduce heat to medium and cook uncovered ~15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until rice is nearly tender.  
6. Add shrimpâ€“chicken mixture and sausage. Continue cooking ~10 minutes more, until meats are cooked and rice is tender.  
7. Season with salt, pepper, and additional Creole seasoning as needed.

## Emeril's Creole Seasoning (Bayou Blast)

- 2 1/2 Tbsp paprika  
- 2 Tbsp salt  
- 2 Tbsp garlic powder  
- 1 Tbsp black pepper  
- 1 Tbsp onion powder  
- 1 Tbsp cayenne pepper  
- 1 Tbsp dried oregano  
- 1 Tbsp dried thyme  

**Mix thoroughly.** Makes ~2/3 cup.
