# Puffy Cakes

**Source:** Kelly

**Cook Time:** 15–20 minutes

Kelly's version of a Dutch baby — a simple puffed oven pancake with crispy golden edges and a custardy centre.

---

## Ingredients

- ½ cup white flour
- ½ cup milk (or milk substitute)
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- ¼ cup butter
- ½ tablespoon vanilla (optional)

---

## Method

1. **Preheat oven:** Preheat oven to 425°F. Place a metal, glass, or ceramic 8-inch pie pan in the oven while it heats, with the butter (cut up) in the pan.

2. **Make the batter:** Whisk the eggs, milk, and sugar together until smooth. Stir in vanilla if using. Let the batter rest for at least 10–15 minutes.

3. **Pour batter:** Once the oven is up to temperature and the butter is melted and starting to bubble — check regularly to make sure it's not burning — pour the batter into the pan. Do not mix the batter with the butter.

4. **Bake:** Bake for 15–20 minutes, or until the centre is baked through and the edges are puffed and golden.

5. **Serve:** Serve with desired toppings: cinnamon and sugar, maple syrup and sweetened yogurt, berries and whipped cream, or fresh lemon juice and brown sugar.
