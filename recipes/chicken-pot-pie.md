# Individual Chicken Pot Pies

**Makes:** 4–6 ramekins (8 oz)
**Prep Time:** 20 min
**Cook Time:** 30–35 min

---

## Ingredients

- **1 lb (about 2 cups)** cooked chicken, cubed or shredded
- **1 tbsp** butter or olive oil
- **1 small onion**, diced
- **2 carrots**, diced
- **2 celery stalks**, diced
- **1/2 cup** green peas (fresh or frozen)
- **3 tbsp** flour
- **1 1/2 cups** chicken broth
- **1/2 cup** milk or cream
- **1/2 tsp** dried thyme
- Salt & pepper, to taste
- **1 sheet** puff pastry, thawed
- **1 egg**, beaten (for egg wash)

---

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven** to 400°F (200°C).

2. **Cook filling:**
   Heat butter or oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add onion, carrots, and celery. Cook 5–6 minutes until softened.
   Stir in peas and cook 1–2 more minutes. Sprinkle in flour and stir constantly for 1–2 minutes.
   Slowly add broth and milk, stirring to avoid lumps. Simmer until thickened (5–6 minutes).
   Add chicken, thyme, salt, and pepper. Remove from heat.

3. **Fill ramekins:**
   Divide the mixture evenly among 4–6 ramekins.

4. **Top with crust:**
   Cut puff pastry to fit just over each ramekin with a little overhang. Press edges down and cut a small slit in the top for steam.

5. **Egg wash:**
   Brush tops with beaten egg.

6. **Bake:**
   Place ramekins on a baking sheet to catch drips. Bake for 25–30 minutes, or until golden brown and bubbly.

7. **Cool:**
   Let sit for 5–10 minutes before serving.
