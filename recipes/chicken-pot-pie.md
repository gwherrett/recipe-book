# Chicken Pot Pie

## Ingredients
- 1 lb chicken, cooked and cubed
- 1/2 cup diced carrot
- 1/2 cup green peas
- 1/4 cup diced celery
- 1/6 cup butter
- 1/6 cup diced onion
- 1/6 cup flour
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp black pepper
- 1/8 tsp celery seed
- 1/2 cup chicken stock
- 1/3 cup milk
- 2 sheets puff pastry

## Preparation
1. In a saucepan, sauté carrot, celery, onion, and peas in butter until soft. Stir in flour, salt, black pepper, and celery seed. Slowly stir in chicken stock and milk. Simmer until very thick.
2. Combine mixture with chicken and wrap in puff pastry.
3. Bake at **375°F** for **45 minutes**, or until internal temperature reaches **75°C**.
